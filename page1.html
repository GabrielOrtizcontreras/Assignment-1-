Outline of some new things I have learned here

  1. First new concept: Nested List
    1. You can sort them anyway.
    2. You can manipulate the fonts and change how they are labeled as well.
  2. Second new concept: Changing Fonts
    1. You can use many different fonts and in many different forms
    2. You can make the font smaller or larger and make many different cool styles.
  3. Third new concept: Making Tables
    1. You can make a neat looking table that can have different colors to make a website look as legitimate for people to look at.
    2. You can list as many rows and columns as you like and they will look great as well as change the fonts within the table itself.
